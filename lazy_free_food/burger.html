burger

Making a mouthwatering hamburger couldn't be simpler with this foolproof method. Just take a pound of ground beef, season it generously with salt and pepper, and form it into four equally-sized patties with a slight dimple in the center to prevent bulging. While your pan or grill heats to medium-high, slice some fresh tomatoes and lettuce, and toast your favorite burger buns until they're golden brown. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness, adding a slice of cheese in the final minute if desired. Layer your burger with classic toppings, and don't forget a generous spread of mayo, mustard, or your favorite sauce. From start to finish, you'll have restaurant-quality burgers ready to serve in just 15 minutes!

Ingedents:

  • 1/4 patty of any meat you want
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Hamburger Seasoning
  • Any toppings that you want on it

Instrucations:

  • put your patty on the pan on high heat
  • flip the patty every minute and put seasoning on each side once
  • flip six times until cooked
  • Put your patty in your buns and enjoy your dinner
