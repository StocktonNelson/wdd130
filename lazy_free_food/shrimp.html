Air Fryer Shrimp

Create perfectly crispy and juicy shrimp in minutes using your air fryer! Start with thawed, peeled, and deveined shrimp. Pat them dry with paper towels to ensure maximum crispiness. Season generously with your favorite seafood seasoning, garlic powder, and a touch of lemon pepper. Preheat your air fryer to 400째F (200째C). Place the shrimp in a single layer in your air fryer basket, making sure not to overcrowd them. Cook for just 5-7 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through, until the shrimp are pink and curled into a loose "C" shape. Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce or over a fresh salad. This quick and easy method delivers restaurant-quality shrimp in under 10 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp (16-20 count)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  • Preheat air fryer to 400째F (200째C)
  • Pat shrimp dry and toss with oil and seasonings
  • Place in single layer in air fryer basket
  • Cook 5-7 minutes, shaking halfway through
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges
